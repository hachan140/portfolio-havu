Summary

When starting our final capstone project at university, we conducted research and surveys on document storage needs in organizations, including companies and schools in Vietnam. We identified a strong demand for a document management system equipped with essential features such as uploading, downloading, viewing, access control, sharing, and, most importantly, fast and accurate document search.

My Contribution

• Conducted research and user surveys on document management system needs, analyzing user feedback through charts to identify weaknesses in existing systems and trends for a new system.

• Defined product feature requirements and formulated detailed Software Requirement Specification (SRS); Outlined both functional and non-functional requirements to ensure system efficiency, usability, and scalability.

• Built backend features such as authentication, organization management, user management, and department management; integrated GPT AI models for semantic and natural language search.

• Planned and structured the project timeline, organized meetings, and coordinated team efforts.

• Ensured the quality of each feature by tracking progress on Jira, providing feedback and suggestions for ongoing developments.
